About Setsuna

2023-10-18
Setsuna
333 words

Setsuna, emerging from the intricate tapestry of Redo of Healer, inhabits a realm where the boundaries of justice and vengeance often intertwine.

The scars of Setsuna’s life serve as testimony to her resilience. Each hardship only serves to forge her spirit ever stronger. Having experienced the cruelty of bondage and witnessed her tribesfolk suffer similarly, a profound animosity towards humans has taken root within her. She revels in exacting revenge upon those who’ve wronged her, yet she draws a clear line, refusing to harm the innocent.

Wielding the Ice Claws, Setsuna’s hands become sheathed in frost, giving rise to lethal talons extending from her fingertips. Her agility is unparalleled, allowing her to outmaneuver and overpower most humans. This prowess has been further amplified due to certain daily actions, enhancing her potential beyond conventional limits.


Amid the silvery moonlit embrace of the Ice Wolf Village, Setsuna stood both fierce and fragile. The echoes of battle, where her enemies met their chilling end by her hands, still rang in the air. Amidst the tranquil forest, she took a moment of vulnerability. With a soft moan and shimmering eyes, she whispered her true name, a name that signified a profound devotion, transcending mere servitude. In that moment, with the name spoken, a promise was made and destinies forever changed.

With silvery tresses flowing like moonlit rivers and striking eyes that mirror the vastness of the azure sky, Setsuna stands as an embodiment of fierce grace. The icy talons that envelop her hand and the cold blade that extends from her ankle manifest her formidable strength. The lupine features – from the tufted ears with their contrasting grey tips to her majestic tail – hint at her unique lineage and connection to the enigmatic Ice Wolf Village.

Adorned in an attire that merges both allure and battle-readiness, her stance radiates a silent determination. A warrior’s spirit, intertwined with a heart that holds secrets as deep as the night, makes Setsuna an unparalleled figure in her world.
